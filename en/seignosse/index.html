Seignosse · Landes

Seignosse: between ocean, pine forest and Landes lakes.

Seignosse (40510), on the Landes coast in south-west France, is one of the finest spots on the Atlantic seaboard. Endless stretches of golden sand, a pine forest heavy with the scent of resin, quiet lakes for slower afternoons and a lively village where it's good to stop and stay a while.

Overview

A seaside resort in the heart of the Landes.

Between Hossegor (2 km south) and Vieux-Boucau (10 km north), Seignosse is a world-famous surf destination — home to the Quiksilver Pro France competition and well-respected surf schools — while still holding on to its down-to-earth Landes village feel.

The town stretches between the more authentic, residential village centre and the coast with its lifeguarded summer beaches. In between: the Landes de Gascogne forest, its maritime pines and its network of cycle paths.

The beaches

The 5 beaches of Seignosse.

Seignosse has around ten kilometres of wild Atlantic coastline. Every beach is 5 to 10 minutes by car from Uhaïna House.

Les Bourdaines

A lifeguarded, family-friendly beach with car park access and seasonal shops. Perfect for families with children.

Les Estagnots

One of the best surf spots on the Landes coast. Host to international surfing competitions.

Le Penon

A long beach with seasonal shops and restaurants. Laid-back atmosphere and a well-known surf spot.

Les Casernes

Wilder and tucked into the pine forest. Ideal for long walks along the sand.

Plage des Chênes Lièges

A peaceful, natural beach reached by a forest path. For those seeking quiet.

Plage de l'Agréou

To the north, more secluded. Perfect for a sunny afternoon away from the crowds.

Activities

What to do in and around Seignosse.

🏄 Surf & bodyboard

World-class spots. Plenty of surf schools for beginners and experienced surfers alike.

🚴 Cycling — Vélodyssée

Safe, dedicated cycle paths connecting Seignosse, Hossegor and Capbreton. Bike hire available locally.

⛳ Golf de Seignosse

18-hole course rated among the finest in Europe, carved through the pine forest.

🏊 Hossegor sea-lake

Calmer swimming, paddle-boarding, pedalos. Just 10 minutes away, perfect for flat-sea days.

🥾 Hiking & nature

The Landes forest, dunes and waymarked trails. Remarkable wildlife and plant life.

🛍️ Seignosse market

Village market featuring local producers: oyster farmers, market gardeners, artisan bakers.

🍷 Landes food & wine

Village restaurants and regional specialities (duck, asparagus, and nearby Espelette pepper).

🎾 Sports & leisure

Tennis, Basque pelota, horse riding, a forest adventure park at Seignosse-Océan.

🌊 Coastal day trips

Hossegor (2 km), Capbreton (5 km), Biarritz and the Basque Country (30 min).

Getting here

How to reach Seignosse.

🚗 By car

A63 motorway, take the Saint-Geours-de-Maremne exit and head for Seignosse (~15 min). Private parking on site.

🚄 By train

TGV stations at Bayonne or Dax (30 min by car). Shuttles and taxis available.

✈️ By plane

Biarritz — Pays Basque airport is 45 minutes away. Car hire available on arrival.
