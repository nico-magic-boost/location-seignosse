The house

130 m² house in the heart of Seignosse — 3 air-conditioned bedrooms.

Uhaïna House is a fully renovated holiday home set right in the centre of Seignosse village (40510), on the Landes coast of south-west France. 130 m² of living space, 3 air-conditioned bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a 50 m² living room and a 60 m² terrace. Designed to sleep up to 6.

Overview

A house built around family life.

We bought Uhaïna House in 2018 and gave it a full renovation. When our children arrived, we extended it so we could live here and share it: 130 m² today, three air-conditioned bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a 50 m² living room that really breathes.

Set right in the village of Seignosse, the house combines the calm of a residential street with the convenience of walking to shops, the market and restaurants. The beaches of Seignosse are a 5-minute drive away, Hossegor 10 and Capbreton 15.

Here you cook together, eat outside, come home from the beach with sand on your feet, and settle on the terrace as the sun goes down. It's our house — and while you're renting it, it becomes yours.
