FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Where exactly is Uhaïna House in Seignosse?

Uhaïna House is at 4 avenue Marcel Cerdan, 40510 Seignosse, right in the heart of the village (Landes, south-west France). Shops, market and restaurants are within walking distance. Seignosse beaches (Les Bourdaines, Les Estagnots, Le Penon) are 5 minutes by car or 10 minutes by bike.

What is the minimum stay?

Minimum stay 15 nights. Long-term stays (28 days or more) are also welcome.

Is the pool available year-round?

No. The private outdoor pool is open from July to the end of August, 24/7. A safety cover is provided. The rest of the year, the house stays cosy thanks to central heating and the fireplace.

How many people can the house sleep?

The 130 m² house has 3 air-conditioned bedrooms + 1 home office, 2 bathrooms and 2 WCs, sleeping up to 6 guests. Perfect for families or groups of friends.

Is the house suitable for babies and young children?

Yes. Uhaïna House is very well equipped for families: cot, travel cot, high chair, baby bath, changing table, children's tableware, fireplace guard and socket covers. Enclosed garden and outdoor playground.

How do I book Uhaïna House?

Booking is handled directly on Airbnb, with secure payment and reviews from previous guests. Check-in is self-service via a secure key box — detailed instructions are sent before your arrival. You can also contact us by email with any pre-booking questions.

Are all the bedrooms air-conditioned?

Yes, all 3 bedrooms have air conditioning, adjustable to the half-degree. Portable fans are also available.

Is there on-site parking?

Yes, free parking on site and also on the street. Access to the house is directly from the road, no significant steps — single-storey home.

Which beaches are easy to reach from the house?

Seignosse beaches — Les Bourdaines, Les Estagnots, Le Penon, Les Casernes and Les Chênes Lièges — are all 5 to 10 minutes by car. Hossegor 10 min, Capbreton 15 min.

Can I get there by train or plane?

Yes. Closest train stations are Bayonne (30 min) and Dax (30 min), both served by TGV. Biarritz — Pays Basque airport is about 45 minutes by car.

Is the house suitable for remote work?

Yes. Uhaïna House has a dedicated workspace in a room with a door, fibre WiFi, Ethernet connection and a portable WiFi router — ideal for alternating beach and remote work.

Are bed linen and towels provided?

Yes, sheets, pillowcases and towels are provided. Washer and dryer (free) on site, plus iron and drying rack.

Are pets allowed?

Please contact us directly via Airbnb before booking for any specific pet requests.
