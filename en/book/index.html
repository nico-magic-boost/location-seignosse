FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Where exactly is Uhaïna House in Seignosse?

Uhaïna House is in the heart of Seignosse village (40510), in the Landes region of south-west France. Shops, the market and restaurants are all within walking distance. The beaches of Seignosse (Les Bourdaines, Les Estagnots, Le Penon) are about 5 minutes by car or 10 minutes by bike.

How many people does the house sleep?

The 130 m² house has 3 air-conditioned bedrooms and 5 beds, sleeping up to 6 guests comfortably. Perfect for a family or a group of friends.

How do I book Uhaïna House?

Booking is handled directly on Airbnb, with secure payment and full access to reviews from previous guests. You can also email us with any questions before you book.

Do all the bedrooms have air conditioning?

Yes, all 3 bedrooms have air conditioning, so you can count on cool nights even in the height of the Landes summer.

Is there parking on site?

Yes, the house has its own private parking. The village of Seignosse is also very easy to get around by bike or on foot.

Which beaches are close to the house?

The beaches of Seignosse — Les Bourdaines, Les Estagnots, Le Penon, Les Casernes and Plage des Chênes Lièges — are all 5 to 10 minutes away by car. Hossegor is 10 minutes away, Capbreton 15.

Can I travel by train or plane?

Yes. The nearest railway stations are Bayonne (30 min) and Dax (30 min), both served by TGV high-speed trains. Biarritz — Pays Basque airport is about 45 minutes away by car.

Is the house suitable for remote work?

Yes. Uhaïna House has fibre WiFi and a dedicated workspace with two desks — perfect for balancing beach days and work.

Are bed linen and towels provided?

Yes, bed linen, pillowcases and towels are all provided. Basic welcome products on arrival.

Are pets allowed?

Please message us directly on Airbnb before booking for any specific requests regarding pets.
