About

Nicolas & Charlène —
between sea and mountains.

We're Nicolas & Charlène, parents of two. For several years we lived in this house in Seignosse — and we still jump at every chance to come back.

Our story

A life between ocean and mountain peaks.

We bought Uhaïna House in 2018, gave it a careful first renovation, and then extended it when our children came along. This house is a family place: we've lived here, hosted our loved ones, and spent whole summers with our feet in the sand.

Over time, we chose a life between sea and mountains — Saint-Lary-Soulan in the Pyrenees in winter and Seignosse on the Landes coast for the rest of the year. Because we can't really choose between the two.

Since we no longer live in the house full time, we decided to share it. Uhaïna House now welcomes families, groups of friends and couples looking for a comfortable, warm home for their holiday in Seignosse.

We stay hands-on, available hosts: for any question before, during or after your stay, we're just a message away. It's our house — and while you're staying, it becomes a little bit yours.
